## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information.

### A. Account and Contact Information

When you create an account or use SLICE, we may collect:

- First and last name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Mailing address
- City, state, and ZIP code
- Login or account credentials
- Subscription tier
- Referral code, if applicable

### B. Debt and Financial Planning Information

SLICE allows you to manually enter information to create a customized debt program. This may include:

- Total debt amount
- Creditor names
- Creditor phone numbers
- Amount owed per creditor
- Settlement target percentages
- Estimated settlement amounts
- Monthly savings amount
- Budget information
- Debt program length
- Credit score input
- Creditor notes
- Settlement progress and status
- Call preparation notes

SLICE does not connect to your bank account or automatically pull your credit report unless we clearly add and disclose that feature in the future.

### C. AI Coach and Negotiation Script Information

If you use AI features such as Zest AI Debt Coach, negotiation strategy suggestions, or customized call scripts, we may collect:

- Messages you send to the AI coach
- AI-generated responses
- Creditor and debt information needed to personalize responses
- Selected script tone or negotiation preferences
- Chat history and related app activity

AI features may use third-party AI providers, such as Anthropic Claude or similar services, through a secure backend system. We do not allow AI providers to directly access your full account unless needed to provide the requested AI feature.

### D. Coaching and Booking Information

If you book coaching, founder coaching with Marc Feinberg, tax advisory, Mastermind sessions, or live creditor call support, we may collect:

- Booking date and time
- Selected coaching topic
- Notes or questions submitted before the session
- Relevant debt summary information
- Session status
- Communication preferences

We do not record coaching calls unless we clearly notify you and receive any required consent.

### E. Subscription and Payment Information

SLICE may offer paid subscriptions or in-app purchases.

Payments for digital subscriptions are processed through Apple App Store, Google Play, RevenueCat, or another authorized payment/subscription provider. We do not store your full credit card number or payment card details.

We may receive limited subscription-related information, such as:

- Subscription tier
- Purchase status
- Renewal status
- Expiration date
- Transaction identifier
- App store receipt or entitlement status

### F. Device, Usage, and Technical Information

We may collect basic technical information to operate and improve the app, such as:

- Device type
- Operating system
- App version
- Crash logs
- Performance data
- Feature usage
- General location based on IP address
- Push notification preferences

We do not use this information to sell your personal information.
