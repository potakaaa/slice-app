Build an Expo React Native app for iOS and Android called SLICE, a DIY debt resolution app with the tagline “Reducing your debt one bite at a time.” Use a clean white-and-orange brand with an orange slice logo that has a bite taken out of it. The app helps users create a customized debt settlement program, track creditors, calculate settlement targets, plan monthly savings, and get AI-powered negotiation guidance so they can reduce debt without paying traditional debt settlement companies around 25% of their debt amount.

Create a simple MVP with onboarding, dashboard, customized debt program setup, creditor list, add/edit creditor, credit score tracker, settlement calculator, monthly savings planner, snowball timeline, AI negotiation strategy, AI customized negotiation script, credit repair page, founder coaching booking with Marc Feinberg, subscription/pricing screen, profile, and legal disclaimer screen. During onboarding, users should enter their debt amount, creditors, target settlement percentage, monthly savings amount, and credit score.

For the customized debt program, each creditor should show four key values: debt amount, automatically calculated settled amount based on the selected percentage, monthly savings amount, and estimated program length. Example: if the user owes Bank of America $10,000 and targets a 50% settlement, the app calculates a $5,000 settlement target. If the user saves $500/month, the program length is about 10 months. The user should be able to adjust settlement percentages like 30%, 40%, 50%, 60%, or 70%, and the timeline should update instantly.

Add AI features where the app suggests negotiation strategies and customized call scripts. Example: for a $10,000 Bank of America debt, the AI may suggest starting with a 30% first offer, then provide a simple call script, what to say, what not to say, and what to ask before paying. Add a Credit Repair page with credit score tracking, credit report checklist, dispute letter placeholder, settlement confirmation checklist, and reminders to keep written proof. Do not guarantee settlement approval or credit score improvement.

Use SMART goals and KISS principles. Each debt should have a specific creditor, measurable balance and settlement target, achievable monthly savings plan, relevant snowball priority, and time-bound target settlement date. Keep the MVP simple with Expo Router, TypeScript, Zustand with local persistence, reusable components, and local mock data. Prepare the architecture for Supabase, RevenueCat/StoreKit subscriptions, and a secure Claude AI backend proxy. Include Free, Silver, Gold, and Platinum tiers, with AI scripts, coaching, Marc Feinberg founder coaching, and live creditor call support as premium or locked upgrade features. Include disclaimers that SLICE is not a law firm, does not provide legal/tax/financial advice, does not guarantee settlements, and users should get agreements in writing before paying creditors.
